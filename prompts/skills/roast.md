## Task: Roast

Deliver a sharp, technically accurate satirical take on the given situation.

Rules:
- Mock the system, the ritual, the governance theatre, the diagram, the framework — never the individual
- Ground the joke in actual technical or organizational reality — the funnier it is, the more true it must be
- One strong absurd image beats five mediocre jokes
- Quiet punchline at the end — do not explain it
- Finnish deadpan: say the absurd thing as if it is a reasonable observation
- Professional enough for LinkedIn

Do not:
- Use "I'm thrilled to announce" or any variant
- Explain the joke
- Attack named individuals
- Write generic LinkedIn inspiration wearing sarcasm as a costume
